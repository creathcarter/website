World Building and Environment Art

 

Lord of the Rings Online Art and Design

 

World Building for Lord of the Rings Online; regions of Southern Mirkwood and Enedwaith/Northern Dunland.

 

I generated the regional heightmaps for Enedwaith, Dunland and surrounding areas. My team collectively developed Enedwaith region layout, themes and appearance. I created assets for three different flavors of landscape foliage, trees and rockforms, developed regional geography, daycycle lighting and atmospheric effects, as well as placed structures and encounters. Contributed to design of regional concept, gameplay, culture and lore as it pertained to the Tolkein universe.

 

 

I also created the heightmap data for the Southern Mirkwood landscape. My team collectively developed Southern Mirkwood as the first post-Moria content expansion. I created assets for three different flavors of landscape foliage, trees and rockforms, developed regional geography, daycycle lighting and atmospheric effects, as well as placed structures and encounters. Contributed to design of regional concept, gameplay, culture and lore as it pertained to the Tolkein universe.

 

Also developed multiple dungeons, caverns, and interior spaces. I am not responsible for the art content below.
