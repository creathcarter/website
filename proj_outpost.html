Outpost

(1999)

A real-time 3D environment created in the Motivate Digital Actor engine with a team of Game Development students in my Junior year of college.

Environment and all props and assets were created in two weeks, and featured pathing AI, and rudimentary interactions.

Total polycount for the full environment is 21,258. 
Dragon Guardian Statues and Character Models authored by Erik Jakobsen and Steve Swink.

Some concept sketches created prior to asset authoring.
