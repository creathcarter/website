When Lemmings Attack

Work Contributed:
Environment Design
Texture Creation (Environment)
Co-Game Design

A parody of the original Lemmings. Players attempted to stop the swams of Lemmings from reaching their goal by strategic placement of traps throughout the environment.
This playable prototype was created using the Motivate Digital Actor development environment.

This project lasted from January 2000 to March 2000.
