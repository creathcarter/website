Outpost

Work Contributed:
Lead Environment Design
Texture Creation (Environments)

A playable Roleplaying/Adventure game prototype created using the Motivate Digital Actor software. The player directed the character throughout the wasteland outpost environment from a third person perspective via the mouse. The prototype showcased working NPC AI and interactivity with the surroundings.

The project cycle lasted from September 1999 to December 1999.
The full outpost environment was created in two weeks.
