Sprinky -Interactive Demo (23.4 MB)

This is a full downloadable webgame in Beta stage development.

This game is a side-scrolling action/puzzle game with a large emphasis on physics. The player controls a slinky-like object through 9 varying stages of difficult terrain in an attempt to reach the goal.

Controls:

Mouse - Controls the extension and center of gravity of the Spring.
R - Reposition Spring at starting point
Esc - Return to menu/Exit game
