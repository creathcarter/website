"Space Station Art Test Environment" (2004):

 

This small environment was created for a realtime environment using a poly/texture budget for a console and PC game.
Goal: Create a room, door and hallway under 1500 polygons with 4 256x256 resolution texturemaps. Light for mood and effect.

Final Polycount: 1454
Textures: 4 256x256 TGAs
