Hohenheim

(2004)

PlayTBD

A fantasy environment portfolio piece created in the Unreal Tournament 2003 engine.

Individual models and their wireframes.

Concept sketches used to plan the environment.

Models created in 3dsMax 5.1
Textures created in Photoshop 7
Audio created in Sound Forge 4.5

This project was created for application to a specific company. Requirements were to create an environment suitable for a persistent world, no polycount / texture limit given and was designed with todays PCs in mind.
Recommended Specs: P3/4 2Ghz, 256MB RAM, GeForce 4, equivalent or greater.

Authoring time was 6 days. All design, static meshes, and nearly all textures were created specifically for this environment. Windmill/Mechanical audio effects created specifically for this environment by the author. All other audio copyright by their respective owners.
